Home To A Quality Chiropractor Perth

Perth Chiro Centre is here to help you by providing you and your family with the very best in chiropractic care.

Established in 2006, Perth Chiro Centre is a modern evidence-based clinic offering high quality patient centered care with no referral needed. Your case will be managed by a registered chiropractor with over 12 years clinical experience. We provide high quality service, competitive pricing and are open 5 days per week with after work hours appointments available.

What Treatment Options are Available?

The range of treatment options available at Perth Chiro Centre that are used in the development of a management plan for your condition includes:

  • Spinal and/ or Limb Manipulation.
  • Spinal and/ or Limb Mobilization.
  • Soft Tissue Therapy (e.g. Trigger Point Massage, Cross Frictional Massage, Graston Technique, Muscle Release Technique, Dry needling and Stretching).
  • Traction and Limited Traction Device known as Flexion Distraction.
  • Active Care (Supervised Rehabilitation Exercise Programs, Home Exercises).
  • Physical Rehabilitation and Advice.
  • Referral to appropriate healthcare professionals for further investigations or co management as deemed appropriate.

How Chiropractic Care Can Help You?

Chiropractic care from our Perth Chiropractic Centre can help provide relief for a range of musculoskeletal conditions including; neck pain, neck related headaches, back pain, lower back pain, spine related arm and leg pain (some causes of sciatica), whiplash neck injuries, spine related shoulder pain and some cases of pain and stiffness caused by osteoarthritis. By relieving these pains and discomforts from your bodies, it allows you to continue on living a healthy and happy lifestyle. As well as providing two convenient locations in Perth, our chiropractic centre is also known to be one of the leading chiropractors in Innaloo & Scarborough, as well as one of the leading chiropractor’s in Duncraig and other Northern Suburbs.

We also provide other services, such as Graston Technique and Skin Dry Needling in Perth. Our skin needling technique, also commonly known as Myofascial Dry Needling is a popular soft tissue therapy that can provide pain relief to the symptomatic muscle area.
